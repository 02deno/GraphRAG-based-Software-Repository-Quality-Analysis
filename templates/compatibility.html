How is the overall score calculated?
Each row is one check with a weight (sums to 100%). The check score is a value between 0% and 100% for that criterion only (not “points earned” yet). The headline percentage is the weighted mean: Σ (weight × check score) / Σ weights, then scaled to 0–100%. Expand any row for what that check measures.
